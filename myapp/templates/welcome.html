Mission Statment and Funding

This website is provided through an institution of law or advocacy to victims of domestic violence to securely store their safety plans The forms contained in this website help the victim quickly plan for: leaving a location, safety during a violent incident, safety in their own residence, safety with a restraining order, safety at work, safety when drugs are being used and emotional health safety. This website is designed for quick access to these forms.

Users will have the ability to get PDF versions of these forms to give to their supporters or organizations. This way organization can help a victim through a violent incident if the organization is called for support.

If your or your organization would like access to this website they simply have: 1. Click the link below. 2. Pay $1 to help keep this website running. 3. Share the link with their Victims so victims can access and use this website free.
